Cover/Album Art Specs



Perfect square with size of at least 3000x3000 pixels and max 6000x6000 pixels in .png or .jpeg (not .tiff). 1400X1400 pixels might be accepted but not by all the stores.

Include Strictly:

  • The name of the main artist (Correctly spelled)
  • The release title (Correctly spelled)

Music Specs

  • WAV/FLAC
  • Sample Rate - 44.1 khz/ 44100hz
  • Compression Rate - 16 bit

    Do Not Include:

  • No websites or social media handles.
  • No references to physical packaging like a CD logo, nor to a digital product.
  • No brands nor advertising.
  • No images from other copyright holders are allowed without written permission.
  • No year nor dates.
  • No store names nor special offers.
  • No barcodes.
  • No tilteld or cropped images.
  • No logos including in the background or on clothing.
